# LEP-17 M1 Outbound Candidate Pipeline

## Objective
Open an outbound engineering candidate pipeline and produce enough qualified responses to schedule the first interview loop.

## Target Profile
- Role scope: software engineer with strong product delivery, API/backend fluency, and ownership mindset.
- Seniority band: mid to senior IC (5+ years preferred).
- Signals required:
  - Evidence of shipping production features end-to-end.
  - Clear ownership in ambiguous environments.
  - Collaboration across product/design or customer-facing teams.
- Exclusion signals:
  - No concrete shipped outcomes in past 18 months.
  - Primarily managerial profile without hands-on implementation.

## Sourcing Channels
- GitHub: active maintainers/contributors in adjacent stacks.
- LinkedIn: targeted boolean search around stack and shipping keywords.
- Warm intros: founder/leadership network and first-degree team referrals.

## Weekly Throughput Targets
- New sourced candidates: 30/week.
- Personalized outbound messages sent: 20/week.
- Positive reply rate floor: 15%.
- Intro call conversion from positive replies: 60%.
- First-loop scheduling target: 3 candidates scheduled.

## Outreach Cadence
- Day 0: personalized outreach.
- Day 4: short follow-up with role context and impact.
- Day 10: final follow-up and close-out.

## Ownership
- Pipeline owner: CTO (LEP-17 assignee).
- Recruit coordination: unassigned; tracked via child issue.
- Interview loop scheduling: engineering + hiring manager panel tracked via child issue.

## Exit Criteria for M1
- At least 60 outbound messages sent.
- At least 9 positive replies.
- At least 3 first-loop interviews scheduled with confirmed panel + time.
