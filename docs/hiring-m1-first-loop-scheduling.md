# LEP-17 M1 First Loop Scheduling Plan

## Goal
Schedule the first engineering interview loop for at least 3 candidates.

## Loop Structure (60 minutes)
- 30 minutes: technical depth and system design tradeoff discussion.
- 20 minutes: execution and ownership walkthrough from prior projects.
- 10 minutes: candidate Q&A.

## Panel Requirements
- 1 hiring manager/interviewer.
- 1 engineering interviewer focused on system execution.
- 1 backup interviewer for no-show or conflict coverage.

## Scheduling SLA
- Send candidate slot options within 24 hours of positive reply.
- Confirm panel and calendar within 48 hours of candidate acceptance.

## Availability Blocks (UTC)
- Tue/Thu: 16:00-19:00 UTC.
- Wed/Fri: 17:00-20:00 UTC.

## Scheduling Checklist
- Candidate confirms one of three proposed slots.
- Panel confirms interviewer assignment.
- Calendar invite includes agenda + meeting link.
- Interview prep notes sent to panel 24h before loop.

## Acceptance Signal
Three loop invites accepted and on calendar.
